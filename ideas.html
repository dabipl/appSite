Customers’ ideas implemented in TimeOFF (in various versions):


1. Ability to add comments and attachments to Requests
The option to add a comment and/or attachments when submitting a leave request.

Activating and deactivating e-mail notifications through e-mail templates.
6. Apply for leave with no available days off
Should have option for applying leave even if there is no remaining days off. This leave transaction should appear in system with red flag where line manager and HR Admin should recommend it before final approval. Ability to submit leave request event if there is no available leave. We propose that in this scenario user should submit unpaid leave.
7. Available leave days summary on main site

Monthly calendar display.
12. Calendar of my absences/holidays on main site

Day structure instead of hours.

Date will be in UK format in all views (dd/mm/yyyy).

Features like delegation, block leave, leave adjustment.

Dividing employees by department.

Wise employee leave transaction.
20. Editing e-email templates

With option to enable/disable.

E-mails notify of leave requests, approvals, rejections, cancellations to applicants, HR managers. E-mail notifications can be turned on and off through the administration panel.
24. Employee can reject the leave application
At any level of approval with an automatic e-mail notification to all parties.
25. Employee Details in Home page
In the Home page, Users can view their Employee details, Last leave details and its status and, Superiors hierarchy (S1, S2 & Functional Head) who can approve Leave. Users can also see their Subordinates leave details. Additional informations on home page. Employee details, superiors, information about approvers for that employee etc.

Exceptional cases in HR policies, such as special leave entitlement for individuals should be available.
29. Full calendar view
30. Generating reports.

As admins can we add historic data for a particular year by uploading.

Administrator can adjust Leave balance of an employee.
36. Leave entitlement verificatio
The ability for every employee to verify independently his or her leave entitlements available.
37. Leave Process
Standard users are not able to see the leave process (who is immediate manager, immediate manager’s manager or final approver. The system will forward, recommend, approve and display the leave process for each employee (to the HR manager and/or administrator). Employees can cancel application at any level of approval.
38. Leave records may printed and/or generated into report
Leave records may be printed out or generated into reports - print action for reports.
39. Leave Request and Selection
Supports whole days and employees can apply for a specific number of hours during one day. Duration is selected from a calendar. Choosing a representative has been removed.
40. Leave Request Display
Leave request form should display employee code, employee name, designation, department, date of joining, date of confirmation, date of last availed leave, past and current leave transaction and respective leave rule and policy and consequently notification email should have the same information, so all level approvers will know these required information about an applicant.
41. Leave Request History View (My Forms)
Applicants can see the history of their own requests along with its status and a calendar view. HR Managers can additionally see a history of leave requests of employees from their department/region. Assistants can see leave requests of employees they are assisting.
